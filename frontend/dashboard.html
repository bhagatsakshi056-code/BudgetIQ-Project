❓ FAQ
How do I edit a transaction? ▾
Go to Transactions page, find the transaction and click the Edit button in the Actions column.
Can I filter transactions by month? ▾
Yes! On the Transactions page, use the "Filter by Month" dropdown to see only that month's data.
What does Total Savings show? ▾
It shows your total Income minus total Expenses — this is your net savings across all time.
How do I set a saving goal? ▾
Go to the Goals page and click "+ Add Goal". Enter the goal name, target amount, and saved amount.
Is my data secure? ▾
Yes. Your data is stored in your own MongoDB database and never shared externally.
